Create a Gmail Account

Visit the Gmail Website

  • Go to the Gmail homepage by entering www.gmail.com in your web browser.

Initiate Account Creation

  • Click on the "Create account" button, which is typically found in the top right corner of the page.
  • You will be prompted to choose between "For myself" or "To manage my business."

Complete the Signup Form

  • Fill in the required fields:
    • First Name: Enter your first name.
    • Last Name: Enter your last name.
    • Username: Choose a unique username for your email address (e.g., username@gmail.com).
    • Password: Create a strong password (at least 8 characters, mixing letters, numbers, and symbols).
    • Confirm Password: Re-enter your password to confirm.

Proceed to the Next Step

  • Click on the "Next" button to continue.

Verify Your Phone Number

  • Google will request verification of your phone number for security purposes.
  • Enter your phone number and click "Next."
  • You will receive a verification code via SMS; enter this code and click "Verify."

Provide Additional Information

  • Recovery Email Address (optional): You can add an alternative email address for account recovery.
  • Date of Birth: Enter your birth date.
  • Gender: Select your gender from the options provided.

Agree to Terms and Conditions

  • Review Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  • Click "I agree" to accept the terms and proceed.

Complete Account Setup

  • Google may offer additional services or settings.
  • You can choose to set these up now or skip them.
  • Click "Next" or "Skip" as appropriate.

Access Your New Gmail Account

  • You will be directed to your new Gmail inbox, where you can start sending and receiving emails.

Tips for a Secure Account

  • Use a Strong Password: Ensure your password is complex to enhance security.
  • Enable 2-Step Verification: This adds an extra layer of protection to your account.
  • Keep Recovery Information Updated: Regularly check that your recovery email and phone number are current.

Create a GitHub Account

Go to the GitHub Website

  • Go to the GitHub website.

Sign Up

  • Click on "Sign up".
  • Enter your email address.
  • Create a password.
  • Choose a username.
  • Verify your email address.
  • Complete the setup steps.
  • You're done! You've created a GitHub account.

How to Clone/Pull a Repository in GitHub

  • Open GitHub and navigate to the repository you want to clone.
  • Click on the "Code" button and copy the URL provided.
  • Open your terminal and navigate to the directory where you want to clone the repo.
  • Type git clone [URL] and press Enter.
  • To pull changes, navigate to the repository folder in your terminal.
  • Type git pull and press Enter.
  • You're done! You've cloned and pulled a repository from GitHub.
